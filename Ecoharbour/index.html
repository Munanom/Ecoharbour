Learn More About Us

Why Sugarcane Paper?

Our sugarcane paper offers a sustainable alternative to traditional wood-based paper, driven by our commitment to environmental stewardship. It's priced 5% lower, ensuring accessibility for all, especially benefiting students and educators without harming the planet.

Empowering Communities

Beyond its environmental benefits, our initiative supports communities by creating jobs in Africa through transforming sugarcane bagasse into paper material. This contributes to local economies, fostering empowerment and prosperity.

Our Vision

We envision a future where sustainability and affordability go hand in hand. By 2030, we aim to significantly advance towards this goal, creating a greener and more inclusive world for future generations.


Join us on our journey towards sustainability. Together, we can make a difference.

SDG 13: Climate Action

By using alternative materials like sugar cane bagasse, which is a byproduct of the sugar industry, we contribute to mitigating deforestation and promoting a more sustainable approach to resource use.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Providing affordable and environmentally friendly paper can contribute to improving access to quality education, especially for students from low-income backgrounds.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

The use of sugar cane bagasse for paper production is a sustainable and responsible choice, as it reduces the dependence on traditional paper made from trees and promotes environmentally friendly manufacturing processes.
